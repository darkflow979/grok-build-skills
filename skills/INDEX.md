## Discovery tips

- Browse this index first.
- Open a skill’s `SKILL.md` for the full procedure.
- From a terminal (optional): search frontmatter with your preferred tool, e.g. look for lines starting with `name:` under `skills/**/SKILL.md`.
- Status meanings: `draft` (under review), `active` (ready to use), `deprecated` (prefer a replacement).
